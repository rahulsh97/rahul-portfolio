bharatIndustryR

An R package that harmonises India’s Annual Survey of Industries (ASI) and Annual Survey of Unincorporated Sector Enterprises (ASUSE) into a clean, research-ready format.

It provides functions to compute industrial cluster metrics (LQ, HHI, productivity), compare formal vs informal enterprises, and generate district × industry × year panels.

The package includes tools for cleaning, aggregation, deflation, and mapping, enabling researchers to explore India’s industrial structure at multiple levels.

BharatViz

An interactive Shiny dashboard that integrates industrial clusters (ASI/ASUSE), labour market inequality and migration (PLFS), and socio-economic deprivation (SECC/NFHS/Census) into one platform.

Users can explore heatmaps, graphs, and bivariate maps, interactively overlay datasets, and identify patterns such as: “Do industrial clusters coincide with deprived districts?” or “Which industries should a district diversify into next?”

The dashboard also features trajectory classification, diversification recommendations, and night-lights validation.
